Creative Achievement

Ranking large language models by their performance on six external creative-achievement benchmarks across three constructs. Per-construct and overall scores are z-means— each benchmark is z-scored within itself across the model pool, then averaged across the construct’s benchmarks the model has data for. A score of +1.00 means the model is one standard deviation above the average pool model on that construct.
