Creativity Tests

Ranking creativity tests by their capability-controlled ability to predict creative-writing, divergent-thinking, and scientific-ideation benchmarks. Each test gets a score per construct: Frontier % = 100 × (v + s) / (v* + s*), where v is raw Pearson r with the benchmark, s is semi-partial r after residualising on a capability stack g = (Arena Overall, MMLU-Pro), and (v*, s*) is the joint optimum on the panel-averaged ceiling.
