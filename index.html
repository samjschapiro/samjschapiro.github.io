Research Areas

Our group explores diverse intersections of AI and physics — from cosmic-scale computations to quantum-inspired deep learning architectures.

Cosmic AI

Training ML models on large-scale astrophysical data to uncover hidden structures and fundamental laws of the universe.

Quantum ML

Investigating how principles of quantum information can inform next-generation AI algorithms, pushing the frontier of computation.

Biophysics & AI Safety

Studying complex emergent behaviors in living systems, plus ensuring advanced AI models behave safely and reliably.
