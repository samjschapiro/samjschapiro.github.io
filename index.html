Founding Research Scientist – Spiral Works

I study the science of intelligence by integrating mathematics, computer science, philosophy, and psychology. Currently, I'm working to develop provably creative AI.

Research Areas

My research explores the theory and practice of creativity in AI, transfer learning, deep learning, and the philosophical foundations of intelligence.

Creativity in AI

Developing frameworks for evaluating the creativity of AI-driven ideas, bridging the gap between computational generation and human originality.

Transfer Learning Theory

Investigating how Sharpness-Aware Minimization (SAM) and other methods can improve out-of-distribution generalization and model robustness.

Philosophical Foundations

Exploring how concepts from metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy inform the design and implications of AI systems.
