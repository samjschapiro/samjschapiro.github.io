Research Focus

My work centers on bridging physics and machine learning— harnessing AI to further our understanding of the universe, while applying physical insights to advance AI safety and efficiency.

In particular, I’m interested in how physical laws can inspire next-generation neural networks, and how advanced AI can deepen our grasp of fundamental cosmic structures.

Biophysics

Investigating how machine learning principles can model complex, emergent behaviors in living systems.

Astrophysics Theory

Using large-scale data and AI approaches to study dark matter, cosmic structures, and the origins of our universe.
